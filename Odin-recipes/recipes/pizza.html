Pepperoni Pizza

Description

The most popular pizza in the world. A delicious combination of tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and pepperoni.

Ingredientes

  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni

Instructions

  1. 1/2 cup of tomato sauce
  2. 150g of mozzarella cheese
  3. 100g of pepperoni sausage
